About This Book

This book is a heartfelt collection of memories, reflections, and lessons from Peter and Masea's journey together. Each chapter provides a unique perspective on their relationship and the emotions we experienced.

Created as a therapeutic exercise and a tribute to what we shared, this book aims to preserve the authenticity of our experience while providing closure and insight.
