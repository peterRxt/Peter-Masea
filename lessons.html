    🌹 Lessons Learnt in Love & Marriage 💍

    ✨ Positives:

    • Love is sweet; butterflies flow in day in, day out 🦋💞
    • Having a company to share and reflect with 🧑‍🤝‍🧑🗣️
    • A child makes it even better — Daddy, Mummy, and endless stories 👨‍👩‍👧📖
    • It brings a sense of responsibility and future commitments 🛠️🗓️
    • Brings family members closer together ❤️👪

    ⚠️ Negatives:

    • Marriage is not easy — more miserable moments than happy ones 😞😅
    • Love is hard to find and rarely equal; one may give 80%, the other 85% 💔📉
    • Marriage, job, and business don’t mix... at all 🔄💼🚫
    • Marriage is a contract — if I don't offer something, the contract ends 📃❌
    • “In sickness and in health, in riches and poverty” — a myth that needs editing 🧾🔧
    • Be financially independent — I should have never left Hallmark to "work at Vimax" 💸🤦‍♀️
    • It's mentally draining at times 🧠💣
    ✅ Verdict: Look for money, stay healthy 💪💰. When the right one comes, past lessons will be measured and wisely applied 🧠❤️.
